About Brandon Media

Our Vision

To be the go-to communication management and growth partner in Africa

Brandon Media is a leading outdoor advertising and communications agency with presence in both Uganda and Kenya. We specialize in different forms of advertisements including ferry branding, billboards, digital screens, and comprehensive communication strategies. From compelling marketing campaigns to managing public relations, we provide strategic advice to clients and use appropriate platforms to improve communication.

Founded by Brenda Adong, a communications expert with over 7 years' experience in Public Relations Management, Brandon Media has become a trusted partner for businesses seeking to enhance their brand presence and communication strategies.

Brenda is also a confidence expert on a mission to empower girls and women to launch, grow and scale businesses. She helps women overcome self-doubt to perform at their best, bringing this empowerment philosophy to every project we undertake.

Our comprehensive approach combines strategic thinking with creative execution to deliver results that exceed expectations. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and delivering tailored solutions that drive real business value.
